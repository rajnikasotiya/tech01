One key challenge in the hiring process is accurately assessing whether a candidate’s practical skills truly match the project’s real-world requirements, especially when resumes may not fully reflect hands-on experience. Another challenge is aligning specialized technical skills (like NLP or sentiment analysis in this case) with the exact business problem. Additionally, evaluating a candidate’s ability to adapt, learn quickly, and collaborate with cross-functional teams can be difficult during initial screening. There is also the risk of bias or overemphasizing certain skills while overlooking others that may be equally important for long-term success.
