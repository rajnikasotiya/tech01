### 1. Step-by-Step Plan

**Problem:** Improving productivity and consistency while managing AI projects, interview preparation, and learning goals.

1. Define clear short-term and long-term goals for learning, project completion, and interview preparation.
2. Create a centralized task tracker using Excel, Notion, or Google Sheets to organize deadlines, milestones, and priorities.
3. Break large projects into smaller weekly and daily tasks to reduce overwhelm and improve execution consistency.
4. Use AI tools like ChatGPT and Code Interpreter to automate repetitive work such as summarization, documentation, report generation, and data analysis.
5. Allocate dedicated time blocks for coding, learning, project work, and revision using a structured weekly schedule.
6. Apply productivity methods such as Pomodoro sessions, distraction-free deep work periods, and priority-based task management.
7. Track progress weekly using metrics such as completed tasks, interview practice hours, project milestones, and learning consistency.
8. Review bottlenecks regularly and adjust schedules or priorities based on workload and performance trends.
9. Build a portfolio of completed AI/NLP projects and maintain updated documentation for interview readiness.
10. Save all plans, progress reports, and generated insights into organized files for future tracking and continuous improvement.
