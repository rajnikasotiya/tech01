I’ve been experimenting with the Coursera course “ChatGPT Code Interpreter by Jules White,” and I’m honestly surprised by how powerful AI becomes when you combine prompts with real-world data files.

Today I uploaded a CSV extracted from Vanderbilt University’s 2022 financial report into Code Interpreter and used a sequence of prompts to automatically analyze the data, create visualizations, discover trends, and even generate presentation-ready insights. What normally takes hours in Excel and PowerPoint happened in minutes.

One of the most interesting findings was how operational growth, admissions selectivity, and financial expansion moved together over time. By visualizing the data with trend lines and automated analysis, it became much easier to see patterns that would have been difficult to notice manually.

The workflow was incredibly simple:

1. Upload the CSV file
2. Ask ChatGPT to explain the columns
3. Request visualizations with trend lines
4. Ask it to save charts as images
5. Generate a PowerPoint automatically

Example prompt:

“Read and explain the data in the uploaded CSV. Create four interesting visualizations with trend lines showing admissions, revenue, and expense patterns over time. Save the charts as images and generate a PowerPoint presentation with one chart per slide.”
