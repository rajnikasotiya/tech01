### 1. Sample Answer

**Problem:** Managing time effectively while balancing AI projects, interview preparation, learning, and personal responsibilities.

1. **Create a Structured Weekly Planning System**
   Use a digital planner or spreadsheet to divide your week into focused blocks for coding practice, project work, interview preparation, exercise, and relaxation. Prioritize high-impact tasks first and assign deadlines to avoid procrastination. Code Interpreter can even help generate optimized schedules based on your workload patterns.

2. **Automate Repetitive Workflows with AI Tools**
   Leverage ChatGPT and Code Interpreter to automate repetitive tasks such as summarizing documents, generating reports, organizing notes, preparing presentations, and analyzing datasets. This reduces manual workload and frees up time for higher-value learning and problem-solving activities.

3. **Apply the “Small Daily Progress” Strategy**
   Instead of waiting for long uninterrupted study sessions, focus on consistent daily improvements. For example, spend 30–60 minutes daily on interview preparation, model tuning, or reading AI papers. Small consistent efforts compound over time and reduce burnout compared to irregular intensive sessions.

4. **Build a Centralized Learning and Project Dashboard**
   Maintain all project tasks, learning goals, certifications, deadlines, and ideas in a single dashboard using Excel, Notion, or Google Sheets. Track progress visually using charts or milestone trackers. This improves clarity, accountability, and motivation.

5. **Reduce Context Switching and Improve Focus**
   Constantly switching between tasks reduces productivity. Use focused work sessions such as the Pomodoro Technique or dedicated “deep work” hours where notifications and distractions are minimized. Group similar activities together, such as coding tasks, meetings, and research reading, to maintain momentum and mental efficiency.
