### Prompt-Based Application: “AI Meeting & Task Summarizer”

One daily problem I face is managing long project discussions, client meetings, and technical conversations. It is difficult to remember all action items, deadlines, and technical decisions after meetings. To solve this problem, I designed a prompt-based application using a Large Language Model called **AI Meeting & Task Summarizer**.

This application takes raw meeting notes, chat messages, or transcripts as input and automatically:

* Summarizes the discussion
* Extracts important action items
* Identifies owners and deadlines
* Creates follow-up email drafts
* Highlights risks or blockers

The application helps improve productivity and reduces the chances of missing important tasks.

---

## Prompt Used

**System Prompt:**

“You are an intelligent project assistant. Analyze the provided meeting notes and generate:

1. A concise meeting summary
2. Key decisions made
3. Action items with owners and deadlines
4. Risks or blockers
5. A professional follow-up email draft

Keep the response clear, structured, and professional.”

---

## Sample Input

“Today the AI claims processing team discussed integrating a new document classification model into the insurance workflow. Rajni will prepare the API integration by Friday. Aman will collect additional training data next week. The PEGA team is still waiting for configuration files from the business team, which may delay deployment. The team agreed to test the model in staging before production release.”

---

## Sample Output

### Meeting Summary

The team discussed integrating a new AI document classification model into the insurance claims workflow. The deployment process and pending dependencies were reviewed.

### Key Decisions

* The model will first be tested in the staging environment.
* Production deployment will happen only after successful validation.

### Action Items

| Task                             | Owner         | Deadline  |
| -------------------------------- | ------------- | --------- |
| Prepare API integration          | Rajni         | Friday    |
| Collect additional training data | Aman          | Next week |
| Share configuration files        | Business Team | Pending   |

### Risks / Blockers

* Deployment may be delayed because the PEGA team is waiting for configuration files from the business team.

### Follow-Up Email Draft

Subject: Meeting Summary – AI Claims Processing Integration

Hello Team,

Today we discussed the integration of the AI document classification model into the insurance workflow. Rajni will complete the API integration by Friday, and Aman will collect additional training data next week.

The PEGA team is currently waiting for configuration files from the business team, which may impact deployment timelines. The model will first be tested in the staging environment before moving to production.

Please complete the assigned tasks as per the timeline.

Best Regards,
Project Team

This prompt-based application demonstrates how Large Language Models can automate project coordination tasks, save time, and improve communication efficiency in real-world environments.
