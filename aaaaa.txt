### 2. Sample Answer

A prompt pattern is a reusable way of structuring instructions to help ChatGPT consistently solve a particular type of problem. Instead of writing completely new prompts every time, you can use a repeatable template that guides the AI through a process step by step. For someone working in AI, NLP, or healthcare analytics, prompt patterns can make document analysis, summarization, and data extraction much faster and more reliable. For example, if you regularly analyze insurance claim documents, you could create a prompt pattern that tells ChatGPT to extract diagnosis information, identify missing fields, summarize approval risks, and generate recommendations automatically from uploaded PDFs.

One useful pattern is the “Persona Pattern,” where ChatGPT is instructed to act like a domain expert. For instance, you could upload patient records or medical policy documents and use a prompt such as: “Act as a healthcare claims analyst. Review the uploaded document, identify approval risks, summarize important medical findings, and generate a structured decision report.” This pattern works well because it gives the AI a clear role, defines the task, and specifies the desired output format.

Another valuable pattern is the “Flipped Interaction Pattern,” where the AI asks questions before completing the task. In your case, ChatGPT could first ask what type of medical claims you are analyzing or what business rules matter most before generating insights. This improves personalization and makes the results more relevant to real-world workflows. These prompt patterns are especially powerful with Code Interpreter because they can combine document reading, data analysis, visualization, and report generation in a single workflow.
